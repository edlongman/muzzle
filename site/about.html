The Scoop is a news collector and summarizer, to put it into an example, say you go away for a few weeks.

Then when you get back, you want to know whats been happening without searching through various news sources

Our website takes however long you've been away for, and finds the news from that timeline, then summarizes it with

a paragraph and a link to the full story
